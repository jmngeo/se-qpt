Medical Device Company - Engineering Roles
==========================================

ROLE: Medical Device Systems Engineer
-------------------------------------
Description: Designs and develops complex medical device systems ensuring safety, efficacy, and regulatory compliance. Leads cross-functional teams through the product development lifecycle.

Responsibilities:
- Define system architecture for medical devices
- Develop system requirements and specifications
- Conduct risk analysis per ISO 14971
- Lead design reviews and technical decision-making
- Ensure design history file (DHF) compliance
- Support regulatory submissions (510(k), PMA)

Required Skills: Medical Device Design, ISO 13485, ISO 14971, IEC 62304, FDA Regulations, Systems Engineering


ROLE: Biomedical Engineer
-------------------------
Description: Applies engineering principles to healthcare and medicine. Develops devices that improve patient outcomes.

Responsibilities:
- Design medical devices and equipment
- Conduct biocompatibility assessments
- Perform clinical testing and validation
- Collaborate with physicians and clinicians
- Analyze physiological signals and data
- Support product usability studies

Required Skills: Physiology, Biomaterials, Signal Processing, Clinical Trials, Usability Engineering


ROLE: Embedded Firmware Engineer
--------------------------------
Description: Develops firmware for medical device embedded systems. Ensures software meets IEC 62304 requirements.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement embedded firmware
- Develop software per IEC 62304 lifecycle
- Conduct unit testing and code reviews
- Debug hardware-software integration issues
- Maintain software documentation
- Support software validation activities

Required Skills: Embedded C, RTOS, IEC 62304, Software V&V, Debugging, Medical Device Standards


ROLE: Quality Engineer
----------------------
Description: Ensures medical devices meet quality and regulatory requirements. Manages quality systems and compliance.

Responsibilities:
- Maintain ISO 13485 quality management system
- Conduct internal audits and CAPA management
- Review design documentation for compliance
- Manage supplier quality
- Support FDA inspections and audits
- Track and analyze quality metrics

Required Skills: ISO 13485, FDA QSR, CAPA, Internal Auditing, Supplier Quality, Statistical Methods


ROLE: Regulatory Affairs Specialist
-----------------------------------
Description: Manages regulatory submissions and ensures compliance with global medical device regulations.

Responsibilities:
- Prepare and submit 510(k) and PMA applications
- Track regulatory requirements globally (FDA, CE, PMDA)
- Review product labeling and IFU
- Coordinate with notified bodies
- Monitor regulatory changes
- Support post-market surveillance

Required Skills: FDA 510(k), CE Marking, MDR, Technical Writing, Regulatory Strategy


ROLE: Verification & Validation Engineer
-----------------------------------------
Description: Plans and executes V&V activities to demonstrate medical device safety and effectiveness.

Responsibilities:
- Develop V&V master plans and protocols
- Execute software and system testing
- Perform usability testing
- Conduct electrical safety testing
- Document test results and deviations
- Support design transfer activities

Required Skills: V&V Planning, Test Protocol Development, IEC 60601, Usability Testing, Statistical Analysis


ROLE: Manufacturing Engineer
----------------------------
Description: Develops manufacturing processes for medical devices. Ensures production meets quality and efficiency targets.

Responsibilities:
- Design manufacturing processes and tooling
- Conduct process validation (IQ/OQ/PQ)
- Implement lean manufacturing practices
- Support production troubleshooting
- Manage equipment qualification
- Drive continuous improvement

Required Skills: Process Validation, Lean Manufacturing, GMP, Statistical Process Control, Equipment Qualification
