Manufacturing Plant - Engineering & Operations Roles
====================================================

PLANT MANAGER
-------------
Overall responsibility for plant operations including safety, quality, production, and cost.

Responsibilities:
- Lead plant operations and P&L
- Ensure safety and environmental compliance
- Drive quality and productivity improvements
- Manage plant budget and headcount
- Develop and coach leadership team
- Interface with corporate functions

Required experience: 10+ years manufacturing, leadership experience


PRODUCTION MANAGER
------------------
Manages daily production operations to meet output, quality, and cost targets.

Responsibilities:
- Plan and schedule production
- Manage production supervisors and teams
- Monitor and improve OEE
- Resolve production issues
- Implement lean manufacturing
- Ensure safety compliance

Required experience: 7+ years production, supervision experience


QUALITY MANAGER
---------------
Leads quality assurance and control activities. Ensures products meet specifications.

Responsibilities:
- Manage quality systems (ISO 9001)
- Lead CAPA and root cause analysis
- Conduct quality audits
- Manage incoming and outgoing inspection
- Drive continuous improvement
- Interface with customers on quality

Required experience: Quality management, statistical methods


MAINTENANCE MANAGER
-------------------
Oversees equipment maintenance to ensure reliability and uptime.

Responsibilities:
- Develop preventive maintenance programs
- Manage maintenance technicians
- Plan and execute shutdowns
- Manage spare parts inventory
- Implement predictive maintenance
- Control maintenance budget

Required experience: Maintenance management, reliability engineering


PROCESS ENGINEER
----------------
Develops and optimizes manufacturing processes for efficiency and quality.

Responsibilities:
- Design and improve manufacturing processes
- Conduct process capability studies
- Implement SPC and process controls
- Support new product introduction
- Troubleshoot process issues
- Document procedures and standards

Required experience: Process engineering, statistical analysis


INDUSTRIAL ENGINEER
-------------------
Optimizes plant layout, workflow, and resource utilization.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct time and motion studies
- Optimize plant layout
- Develop standard work instructions
- Analyze capacity and staffing
- Lead kaizen events
- Implement ergonomic improvements

Required experience: Industrial engineering, lean/six sigma


PRODUCTION SUPERVISOR
---------------------
First-line supervisor managing production operators on shift.

Responsibilities:
- Supervise production operators
- Ensure daily production targets
- Maintain safety and housekeeping
- Train and develop operators
- Complete shift reports
- Escalate issues appropriately

Required experience: Production experience, leadership skills


MAINTENANCE TECHNICIAN
----------------------
Performs hands-on maintenance and repair of plant equipment.

Responsibilities:
- Execute preventive maintenance
- Troubleshoot and repair equipment
- Install new equipment
- Maintain maintenance records
- Support production operations
- Follow safety procedures

Required experience: Mechanical/electrical maintenance, troubleshooting


QUALITY TECHNICIAN
------------------
Performs inspection and testing to verify product quality.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct incoming material inspection
- Perform in-process checks
- Execute final product testing
- Document inspection results
- Calibrate measurement equipment
- Report non-conformances

Required experience: Inspection methods, measurement equipment


CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT ENGINEER
-------------------------------
Drives operational excellence through lean and six sigma methodologies.

Responsibilities:
- Lead improvement projects
- Train teams on lean/six sigma
- Facilitate kaizen events
- Track and report improvement metrics
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Sustain implemented improvements

Required experience: Lean Six Sigma certification, project leadership
