Engineering Consulting Firm - Role Descriptions
===============================================

ROLE: Senior Systems Engineering Consultant
-------------------------------------------
Description: Provides expert systems engineering consulting to diverse clients. Leads complex projects and mentors junior consultants.

Responsibilities:
- Lead systems engineering engagements for clients
- Conduct systems architecture design and reviews
- Develop SE processes and tailoring guidance
- Perform capability assessments and gap analysis
- Train client teams on SE best practices
- Support business development and proposals

Required Skills: Systems Engineering, SE Standards (ISO 15288, EIA-632), Consulting, Training, MBSE


ROLE: Technical Project Manager
-------------------------------
Description: Manages technical consulting projects from initiation to closure. Ensures delivery meets client expectations.

Responsibilities:
- Plan and manage consulting projects
- Lead client meetings and workshops
- Coordinate team resources and deliverables
- Track project schedule and budget
- Manage project risks and issues
- Ensure quality of deliverables

Required Skills: Project Management, Client Management, Technical Leadership, Risk Management, Agile/Traditional PM


ROLE: Process Improvement Consultant
------------------------------------
Description: Helps organizations improve their engineering processes. Conducts assessments and implements improvement initiatives.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct process maturity assessments (CMMI, SPICE)
- Identify process improvement opportunities
- Develop and deploy improved processes
- Train staff on new processes
- Measure process effectiveness
- Support organizational change management

Required Skills: CMMI, ASPICE, Process Mapping, Change Management, Training, Metrics


ROLE: Requirements Management Specialist
----------------------------------------
Description: Provides expertise in requirements engineering tools and processes. Implements RM solutions for clients.

Responsibilities:
- Implement requirements management tools (DOORS, Polarion)
- Develop requirements processes and templates
- Train teams on requirements best practices
- Conduct requirements quality reviews
- Establish traceability approaches
- Support tool integration

Required Skills: DOORS, Polarion, Requirements Engineering, Tool Administration, Training


ROLE: Model-Based Systems Engineer
----------------------------------
Description: Applies model-based methods to systems engineering challenges. Develops and implements MBSE solutions.

Responsibilities:
- Develop system models using SysML/UML
- Implement MBSE tools and methods
- Create modeling standards and libraries
- Train teams on MBSE practices
- Conduct model-based reviews
- Integrate models with other tools

Required Skills: SysML, Cameo/MagicDraw, MBSE Methods, Modeling Standards, Tool Integration


ROLE: Safety & Reliability Consultant
-------------------------------------
Description: Provides expert guidance on system safety and reliability. Supports clients in meeting safety standards.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct safety assessments (FHA, FMEA, FTA)
- Support safety certification activities
- Perform reliability analysis and predictions
- Develop safety management plans
- Train teams on safety methods
- Review designs for safety compliance

Required Skills: System Safety, FHA/FMEA/FTA, Safety Standards (MIL-STD-882, ISO 26262), Reliability Engineering


ROLE: Junior Consultant
-----------------------
Description: Supports senior consultants on client engagements. Develops expertise through mentored assignments.

Responsibilities:
- Support senior consultants on projects
- Conduct research and analysis
- Prepare presentations and reports
- Participate in client meetings
- Document findings and recommendations
- Develop technical skills

Required Skills: Technical Background, Communication, Research, Documentation, Teamwork
