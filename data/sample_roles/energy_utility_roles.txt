Energy & Utilities Company - Engineering Roles
==============================================

ROLE: Power Systems Engineer
----------------------------
Description: Designs and analyzes electrical power systems including generation, transmission, and distribution. Ensures reliable power delivery.

Responsibilities:
- Perform power system studies (load flow, short circuit, stability)
- Design substation and transmission systems
- Conduct protective relay coordination
- Support grid integration of renewables
- Analyze power quality issues
- Develop system expansion plans

Required Skills: Power Systems Analysis, ETAP/PSS/E, Protection Engineering, Transmission Design, Grid Integration


ROLE: Renewable Energy Engineer
-------------------------------
Description: Develops renewable energy projects including solar, wind, and energy storage. Optimizes clean energy systems.

Responsibilities:
- Design solar PV and wind systems
- Conduct resource assessment and energy modeling
- Develop project feasibility studies
- Support procurement and construction
- Optimize plant performance
- Monitor and analyze production data

Required Skills: Solar PV Design, Wind Energy, Energy Storage, PVsyst, Project Development, Grid Connection


ROLE: Grid Integration Engineer
-------------------------------
Description: Integrates distributed energy resources with the electrical grid. Ensures stability and compliance with grid codes.

Responsibilities:
- Develop grid integration solutions
- Perform interconnection studies
- Design power electronics and controls
- Ensure grid code compliance
- Model and simulate grid behavior
- Support commissioning activities

Required Skills: Grid Codes, Power Electronics, Inverter Technology, Grid Modeling, SCADA, Protection


ROLE: Asset Management Engineer
-------------------------------
Description: Manages lifecycle of power system assets. Optimizes maintenance strategies and investment decisions.

Responsibilities:
- Develop asset management strategies
- Conduct condition-based assessments
- Optimize maintenance programs
- Analyze asset performance data
- Support capital planning
- Manage asset information systems

Required Skills: Asset Management, Reliability Engineering, Condition Monitoring, Data Analytics, Financial Analysis


ROLE: Protection & Control Engineer
-----------------------------------
Description: Designs protection and control systems for power systems. Ensures rapid fault isolation and system stability.

Responsibilities:
- Design protection schemes
- Set and coordinate protective relays
- Develop control system logic
- Conduct relay testing and commissioning
- Analyze protection events
- Update protection settings

Required Skills: Protective Relaying, SEL/ABB/Siemens Relays, SCADA, IEC 61850, Testing Tools


ROLE: Substation Engineer
-------------------------
Description: Designs high voltage substations including layout, equipment selection, and integration.

Responsibilities:
- Design substation layouts
- Select transformers, breakers, and switchgear
- Develop physical and electrical designs
- Create construction drawings
- Support substation commissioning
- Ensure safety compliance

Required Skills: Substation Design, High Voltage Equipment, AutoCAD, Construction Management, Safety Standards


ROLE: Energy Management Analyst
-------------------------------
Description: Monitors and optimizes energy system operations. Develops strategies for efficient energy use.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor energy system performance
- Analyze energy consumption patterns
- Develop energy efficiency initiatives
- Support demand response programs
- Report on sustainability metrics
- Coordinate with operations teams

Required Skills: Energy Analytics, Energy Efficiency, Demand Response, Data Visualization, Sustainability Reporting
