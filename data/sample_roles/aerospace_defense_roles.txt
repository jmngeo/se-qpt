Aerospace & Defense Organization - Engineering Roles
=====================================================

ROLE: Systems Integration Engineer
----------------------------------
Description: Integrates complex aerospace systems including avionics, propulsion, and structures. Ensures all components work together as a unified system.

Responsibilities:
- Lead system integration activities across multiple disciplines
- Define and manage interface control documents (ICDs)
- Conduct integration testing at various levels (unit, subsystem, system)
- Resolve integration issues and technical anomalies
- Support flight test and certification activities
- Coordinate with subcontractors and suppliers

Required Skills: Systems Integration, MIL-STD, DO-178C, Interface Management, Integration Testing, V&V


ROLE: Requirements Engineer
---------------------------
Description: Captures, analyzes, and manages requirements throughout the system lifecycle. Ensures traceability from customer needs to verification.

Responsibilities:
- Elicit and document stakeholder requirements
- Decompose system requirements to subsystem level
- Maintain requirements traceability matrix
- Conduct requirements reviews and walkthroughs
- Manage requirements changes
- Support verification planning

Required Skills: DOORS, Requirements Analysis, Traceability, Change Management, Technical Writing


ROLE: Safety Engineer
---------------------
Description: Ensures system safety through hazard analysis, risk assessment, and safety certification. Complies with industry safety standards.

Responsibilities:
- Conduct hazard identification and risk assessment
- Perform FHA, FMEA, and FTA analysis
- Develop safety requirements and design solutions
- Support safety certification activities
- Review designs for safety compliance
- Maintain safety case documentation

Required Skills: MIL-STD-882, ARP 4761, Hazard Analysis, FTA/FMEA, Safety Certification, Risk Management


ROLE: Mission Systems Engineer
------------------------------
Description: Designs and integrates mission-critical systems including sensors, weapons, and C2 systems. Ensures operational effectiveness.

Responsibilities:
- Define mission system architecture and requirements
- Integrate sensors, effectors, and data links
- Conduct mission effectiveness analysis
- Support operational testing and evaluation
- Coordinate with military customers
- Manage technical performance measures

Required Skills: Radar/EW Systems, C4ISR, Mission Planning, Operational Analysis, Customer Interface


ROLE: Avionics Engineer
-----------------------
Description: Designs and develops avionics systems including flight computers, displays, and communication systems.

Responsibilities:
- Design avionics hardware and software architecture
- Develop flight management and navigation systems
- Integrate cockpit displays and controls
- Conduct avionics integration testing
- Support DO-178C/DO-254 certification
- Troubleshoot avionics anomalies

Required Skills: DO-178C, DO-254, ARINC 429, MIL-STD-1553, Avionics Design, Flight Software


ROLE: Program Systems Engineer
------------------------------
Description: Provides technical leadership across the program. Manages technical baseline, reviews, and engineering processes.

Responsibilities:
- Lead technical reviews (SRR, PDR, CDR)
- Manage program technical baseline
- Coordinate across engineering disciplines
- Monitor technical performance and risks
- Support proposal and capture activities
- Interface with customer technical team

Required Skills: Technical Leadership, Program Management, Technical Reviews, EIA-632, Risk Management


ROLE: Reliability Engineer
--------------------------
Description: Ensures system reliability through analysis, testing, and design improvements. Predicts and tracks reliability metrics.

Responsibilities:
- Perform reliability predictions and analysis
- Conduct FMECA and reliability block diagram analysis
- Support reliability testing and data analysis
- Track failure data and calculate reliability metrics
- Recommend design improvements
- Support logistics planning

Required Skills: MIL-HDBK-217, FMECA, Reliability Testing, Statistical Analysis, Weibull Analysis
