Industrial Automation Company - Role Descriptions
=================================================

ROLE: Control Systems Engineer
------------------------------
Description: Designs and implements industrial control systems for manufacturing and process industries. Develops PLC and DCS solutions.

Responsibilities:
- Design control system architectures
- Program PLCs and DCS controllers
- Develop HMI/SCADA interfaces
- Conduct factory acceptance testing
- Commission control systems at customer sites
- Troubleshoot control system issues

Required Skills: PLC Programming (Siemens, Allen-Bradley), DCS, SCADA, Ladder Logic, Process Control


ROLE: Automation Engineer
-------------------------
Description: Develops automation solutions to improve manufacturing efficiency. Integrates robots, sensors, and control systems.

Responsibilities:
- Design automated production systems
- Program industrial robots
- Integrate vision systems and sensors
- Optimize production line performance
- Conduct cycle time analysis
- Support production ramp-up

Required Skills: Robot Programming, Vision Systems, PLC, CAD, Lean Manufacturing, OEE Analysis


ROLE: Electrical Design Engineer
--------------------------------
Description: Designs electrical systems for industrial equipment. Creates schematics, panel layouts, and documentation.

Responsibilities:
- Design electrical schematics and panel layouts
- Select electrical components (motors, drives, sensors)
- Create bills of materials
- Develop wiring diagrams
- Support panel manufacturing
- Ensure compliance with electrical codes

Required Skills: EPLAN, AutoCAD Electrical, Motor/Drive Selection, Electrical Codes (NEC, IEC), Panel Design


ROLE: Mechatronics Engineer
---------------------------
Description: Integrates mechanical, electrical, and software systems. Develops smart machines and automated equipment.

Responsibilities:
- Design mechatronic systems
- Integrate sensors and actuators
- Develop motion control solutions
- Prototype and test designs
- Optimize system performance
- Support product development

Required Skills: Mechanical Design, Embedded Systems, Motion Control, Sensors, CAD, Simulation


ROLE: Field Service Engineer
----------------------------
Description: Provides on-site technical support for installed automation systems. Performs maintenance and troubleshooting.

Responsibilities:
- Install and commission automation systems
- Perform preventive maintenance
- Troubleshoot and repair equipment
- Train customer operators
- Provide technical support
- Document service activities

Required Skills: PLC Troubleshooting, Electrical Systems, Mechanical Systems, Customer Service, Technical Documentation


ROLE: Application Engineer
--------------------------
Description: Develops application-specific solutions for customers. Bridges technical capabilities with customer requirements.

Responsibilities:
- Understand customer application requirements
- Develop technical solutions and proposals
- Configure and customize products
- Conduct demonstrations and training
- Support sales team technically
- Create application documentation

Required Skills: Application Development, Technical Sales, Customer Interface, Product Knowledge, Presentations


ROLE: SCADA/MES Engineer
------------------------
Description: Develops supervisory control and manufacturing execution systems. Integrates plant floor data with enterprise systems.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement SCADA systems
- Develop MES solutions
- Create data collection and reporting systems
- Integrate with ERP systems
- Optimize production visibility
- Ensure cybersecurity compliance

Required Skills: SCADA Platforms, MES, SQL Databases, OPC, Data Analytics, Industrial Cybersecurity
