Systems Engineering Department - Complete Role Catalog
=======================================================

This document describes all roles within the Systems Engineering department.


1. CHIEF SYSTEMS ENGINEER
-------------------------
The Chief Systems Engineer is responsible for overall technical direction and systems engineering strategy across all programs. Reports to VP of Engineering.

Primary Duties:
- Set SE vision, strategy, and standards
- Lead technical reviews for major programs
- Approve system architectures and key decisions
- Manage SE resource allocation
- Interface with executive leadership
- Drive SE process improvement

Qualifications: 15+ years SE experience, ESEP/CSEP certification preferred


2. PRINCIPAL SYSTEMS ENGINEER
-----------------------------
Senior technical expert who leads complex system development efforts. Acts as technical authority for assigned programs.

Primary Duties:
- Lead system architecture development
- Chair technical review boards
- Mentor senior staff
- Resolve complex technical issues
- Support proposals and captures
- Define technical standards

Qualifications: 12+ years experience, deep domain expertise


3. SENIOR SYSTEMS ENGINEER
--------------------------
Experienced practitioner who leads subsystem or component development. Works independently with minimal supervision.

Primary Duties:
- Develop system and subsystem requirements
- Create architecture models and documentation
- Lead integration and verification activities
- Conduct trade studies
- Manage technical interfaces
- Present at design reviews

Qualifications: 8+ years experience, strong analytical skills


4. SYSTEMS ENGINEER
-------------------
Journey-level position performing core SE activities. Works under guidance of senior staff.

Primary Duties:
- Document requirements and specifications
- Perform analysis and modeling
- Support integration testing
- Maintain traceability
- Create technical documents
- Participate in reviews

Qualifications: 3-7 years experience, engineering degree


5. ASSOCIATE SYSTEMS ENGINEER
-----------------------------
Entry-level position for recent graduates. Learning fundamental SE practices.

Primary Duties:
- Support requirements documentation
- Assist with testing activities
- Learn SE tools and methods
- Maintain databases and documents
- Attend training programs
- Shadow senior engineers

Qualifications: BS in Engineering, 0-3 years experience


6. REQUIREMENTS ENGINEER
------------------------
Specialist in requirements engineering processes, tools, and best practices.

Primary Duties:
- Manage requirements in DOORS/Polarion
- Ensure requirements quality
- Maintain traceability matrices
- Support requirements reviews
- Train teams on RE practices
- Administer RM tools

Qualifications: Requirements engineering expertise, tool certification


7. V&V ENGINEER
---------------
Focused on verification and validation planning and execution.

Primary Duties:
- Develop V&V plans and procedures
- Define test cases and acceptance criteria
- Conduct verification testing
- Analyze test results
- Document compliance evidence
- Support certification activities

Qualifications: V&V experience, test methodology expertise


8. INTEGRATION ENGINEER
-----------------------
Specializes in system integration across hardware, software, and interfaces.

Primary Duties:
- Plan and execute integration activities
- Manage interface control documents
- Conduct integration testing
- Resolve integration issues
- Coordinate across teams
- Support factory/field integration

Qualifications: Integration experience, multi-discipline background


9. MBSE SPECIALIST
------------------
Expert in model-based systems engineering tools and methods.

Primary Duties:
- Develop system models in SysML
- Create modeling standards and libraries
- Train teams on MBSE practices
- Integrate models with other tools
- Support model-based reviews
- Advance MBSE adoption

Qualifications: SysML expertise, Cameo/MagicDraw proficiency


10. TECHNICAL WRITER
--------------------
Creates and maintains technical documentation for SE artifacts.

Primary Duties:
- Write and edit technical documents
- Ensure document quality and consistency
- Manage document control
- Create templates and standards
- Support proposal writing
- Maintain document repositories

Qualifications: Technical writing experience, engineering background preferred
